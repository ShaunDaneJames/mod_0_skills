Class Bartender

Attributes:
bar_clean: (boolean)
servings_of_whiskey: (integer)
max_beer_quantity: (integer)
bar_open: (boolean)

Methods:
clean_bar: (sets bar_clean boolean to true)
serve_whiskey: (servings of whiskey - 1)
tap_beer: (beer quantity + 100)
close_bar: (sets bar_open boolean to false)
